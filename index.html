The concept

A festival built like a machine you can walk into.

TECHFEST isn't a lecture hall with better lighting. It's a working system - three days of competitions, builds, and talks, wired together like the reactor spinning behind this page. Every section you scroll through changes its state. Nothing here is decorative; everything runs.
